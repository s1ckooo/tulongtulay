Non-Profit Financial Aid Organization


Find a list of national non-profit organizations and charities that will provide financial help or free material items. Charities, including some near you, will oftern be able to help with rent, housing, offer eviction prevention services or free food, and utility bill assistance.


Funding is always limited, and many of theses non-profit agencies rely heavily on donations from individuals and businesses in the local community. Financial assistance is also issued with conditions, and is usually offered as a last resort when a family is faced with a crisis. The demand for help is very high, and since resources are limited, many non-profit organizations prioritize programs for families with children, senior, single mom and the disabled. In addition, charities and non-profit organizations can also help by directing low-income families or the poor to other financial assistance programs.

Angat Buhay


"Angat Buhay" is a program initiated by the Office of the Vice President of the Philippines. It was founded and officially launched on July 1, 2022, a day after its founder Leni Robredo's term as Vice President of the Philippines expired. The program aims to address poverty and improve the living conditions of Filipinos through various projects and initiatives. It focuses on collaborating with different sectors, including the government, private organizations, and non-governmental organizations (NGOs), to create positive and sustainable changes in communities.


Fairplay For All Foundation


The Fairplay For All Foundation (FFA) is a non-stock, non-profit, non-government organization working in the community of Payatas in the Philippines. The Fairplay for All Foundation is dedicated to levelling the playing field; creating opportunities for the children of Payatas to holistically and sustainably break the cycle of poverty. The charity operates on principles of fairness and equality in person. Their mentoring program develops physical and mental health, provides social and emotional support, and creates financial and academic opportunities.


Andres Soriano Foundation Inc.


The Andres Soriano Foundation, Inc. (ASF) is a non-stock, non-profit foundation established in 1968 that has provided integrated area development programs for island communities in Northeastern Palawan, established partnerships with individuals, groups and communities in carrying out health, environment, education and livelihood programs, pioneered and sustained cancer research initiatives and support mechanisms for cancer training in the country, and assisted in relief responses for affected communities during disasters and calamities.



Bukas Palad


Bukas Palad Foundation of the Philippines, Inc. (BP) is a non-profit and non-governmental organization that provides sustainable development, education, livelihood, information and communication strategy to children, elderly, urban poor, and victims of calamities and disasters.Our services are channeled through various programs that seek to serve our beneficiaries in the areas of Education, Livelihood and Healthcare. Bukas Palad Foundation has a solid experience in partnering with individuals and groups to extend to its growing community


Gawad Kalinga


Gawad Kalinga is a Philippine-based movement that aims to alleviate poverty and build communities by fostering a spirit of volunteerism and social entrepreneurship. The name "Gawad Kalinga" translates to "to give care" in English. The organization was founded in 2003 by Antonio Meloto, and it has since grown into a widely recognized and influential force in the Philippines. The primary focus of Gawad Kalinga is on building homes for marginalized communities, particularly those living in slums or informal settlements.


Urban Program for Livelihood Finance and Training


UPLiFT stands for Urban Program for Livelihood Finance and Training. It was created in 1993 by Inter Aide of France, They are registered with the Philippine Securities and Exchange Commission as a non-profit, non-political, and non-sectarian organization based in the Philippines. UPLIFT is a member of the Filipino Association of Foundations. They empower communities through responsive and innovative microfinance, skills training, business and social development services for the urban poor.
